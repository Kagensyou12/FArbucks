Our History

It all started in 1990, when the owner inherited a coffee plantation from his grandfather after his death. This field is located in Bali, more specifically in Bangli Regency Kintamani. By prioritizing the involvement of the surrounding community, this field is well managed from sprout to care and harvest to post-harvest and is a pioneer of the first variety and we are very proud of it. The field of Kintamani is not only focused on high attention to quality, but the manufacturing process before it is ready for consumer consumption is well calculated. Product technology uses state-of-the-art technology to produce the best coffee taste, package and maintain quality. our vision is clear, and that is to supply the best quality coffee products. In May 2022, the first outlet of Farbucks was opened in Jakarta. The name FArbucks was chosen to answer the desire of coffee lovers for highquality coffee products. which is produced from his field in Bali, is the first single origin offered.
